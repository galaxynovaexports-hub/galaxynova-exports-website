Storage & Cooking Guide

Enjoy the Best Aroma & Taste

Proper storage preserves the natural aroma of Gobindobhog rice. Follow these simple recommendations for the best cooking experience.

📦

Storage

  • Store in a cool & dry place.
  • Keep away from direct sunlight.
  • Keep the pouch tightly sealed after opening.
  • Transfer to an airtight container for long-term freshness.
🍚

Cooking

  • Rinse gently 2–3 times before cooking.
  • Recommended ratio: 1 cup rice : 2 cups water.
  • Allow the rice to rest for 5 minutes after cooking.
  • Serve hot for the finest aroma.
👨‍🍳

Perfect For

  • Traditional Bengali Pulao
  • Payesh
  • Khichuri
  • Special Festive Meals
