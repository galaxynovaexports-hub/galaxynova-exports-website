STELORA™ is the premium rice brand of Galaxynova Exports & Trading (OPC) Private Limited, incorporated in West Bengal, India. Just as the Northern Lights illuminate the sky with celestial energy, STELORA carries the luminous essence of Bengal's finest rice to the world.

We specialise in the export of GI-certified non-basmati rice — foremost among them Tulaipanji, a rare, fragrant, short-grain rice with Geographical Indication status from the Government of India. Sourced directly from farmers in South 24 Parganas and the rice belts of West Bengal, each grain carries the provenance of centuries of cultivation.

STELORA is Non-GMO, HACCP-compliant, free from preservatives, and packed to the highest export standards — ready for the most demanding international markets in Europe, the UK, and the UAE.

Tulaipanji

Grown in the alluvial soil of West Bengal, Tulaipanji is a short-grain, intensely aromatic rice awarded Geographical Indication protection by the Government of India.
